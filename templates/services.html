MockInterview

1. MockInterview Steps

Step1 - Get Introduced

After joining a member,log in and tell us about your personal information and target occupation.

Step2 - Conduct an Interview Online

Press the "Get Started" button to start the mock interview. After entering the interview system, you must first check the equipment to test that the microphone and camera of your device are working properly.Then you can start the interview. There will be a total of ten questions in the interview. We will display related questions based on your chosen occupation. The questions will be divided into easy, medium, and difficult. Different level will give different limits on the number of seconds to answer.If you have answered the question within the time limit, you can press the "Next" button to enter the next question.After completing all questions, it will automatically jump to the feedback page.

Step3 - Get Immediate Feedback

We will give you a feedback based on your performance after interview. The report include analysis and suggestions on your emotional interpretation during the interview, suggested answers of the questions, and your competitiveness assessment. It will help you get improved.

2. MockInterview Service

1 Phonetic question

Use speech synthesis technology to read out the text of the title, making you seem to be talking to the interviewer.

2 Question Bank

The question bank includes professional knowledge, skills, motivation and other aspects. Through AI calculations, the corresponding questions are provided for you to reply.

3 Emotion Recognition

Apply an unique AI expression model to identify any emotional changes during the interview and provide you with reference in the final feedback.

4 Speech to Text

Use a speech recognition system to convert speech into text, and build models through word segmentation and syntax analysis to analyze the connection between words in sentences.

5 Semantic interaction

Understand your answer through NLP, and then give you other related questions.

6 Data Analysis

Perform answer analysis and sentiment analysis based on the interview results, and provide you with a competitiveness report.

7 Video Storage

Provide interview video and audio storage function, allowing users to review their interview performance and adjust it by playing back the video.
